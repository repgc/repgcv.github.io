Welcome

Hi, my name is Rebecca, and I am currently a senior at Mizzou studying Textile and Apparel Management with an emphasis in Marketing and Merchandising. Also, I am receiving a minor in Business and a certificate in Information Systems. I will be graduating Mizzou in Spring 2022 with my bachelor’s of science degree from the College of Arts and Science.


I realized I wanted to pursue a degree in fashion because I was always interested into figuring out what colors/ textures went together best. Also, when I was younger, I would help my older sister pick out what to wear, which made me realize it is a passion of mine. As I got older, I continued to gravitate toward fashion. Since I am interested in fashion, I have read about many different fashion designers. My favorite quote is from Coco Chanel.

“In order to be irreplaceable, one must be different”.
